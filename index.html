  • Doxycycline Skin, respiratory infections · versatile
  • Cephalexin Keflex · skin & soft tissue infections

  • Clindamycin Skin and soft tissue infections

Anticoagulants

Most Common Blood thinners

Used to prevent blood clots, reduce the risk of stroke and other clot-related conditions.

  • Warfarin Long-term prevention · INR monitoring required
  • Apixaban Eliquis · stroke prevention
  • Rivaroxaban Xarelto · DVT/PE treatment
  • Dabigatran Pradaxa · direct thrombin inhibitor

  • Clopidogrel Plavix · antiplatelet
